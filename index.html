Online shop

Website for the «Dona» brand. The company is a major supplier of artesian water in the Volgograd market. The main purpose of the website was to develop a quick water ordering system

Tasks

Since we already had an extensive customer base, and there was a task to attract new customers, the primary task was the need to develop a form of quick order in two forms. For new customers with quick registration and an advantageous first order offer, and for regular customers with frequently ordered items

Stylistic solution

Natural artesian water, as a product, is associated with purity and transparency, which was the main factor for the choice of primary colours: as a base of light blue and ringing inky blue, the accent of bright crimson

Outline of site structure with the ultimate goal to inspire a new visitor to become a customer of the company by ordering water and to simplify the algorithm of ordering for regular customers

Quick order form for new customers with quick registration and an attractive offer

An important element of the promotions page. The site visitor's attention is drawn to the attractive offer, enticing them to order in the shortest time possible

A clickable benefits block allows the visitor to verify the quality of the water by going to a detailed description of the water's qualities

A list of renowned organisations that trust the products reinforces what you have read above about the quality of the water

Another form encourages the visitor to think about ordering water with a good deal by subscribing to the newsletter

The range

A user-friendly, intuitive orientation in the catalogue helps customers find their way around. Clear illustrations of the product sections contribute to this task
